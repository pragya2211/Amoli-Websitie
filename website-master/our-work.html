Sangeeta Rawal

Trustee

Sangeeta Rawal, a Trustee at Amoli - a PGT at DPS - Vasant Kunj, New Delhi since 2000 who wants to utilise her dynamic spectrum of advanced technical skills in Computer Science as well as her natural abilities to motivate and communicate kids. She started my career as a teacher in an Institute where she taught students of different semesters CDAC Govt. of India and worked as a technical writer and had written books in Informatics Practices for classes XI & XII as per CBSE syllabus. Kriti Gandhi

Settlor

Kriti Gandhi, the settlor of Amoli Trust is currently pursuing Bachelors degree in Technology and did her schooling from St. Thomas’ school, Mandir Marg.
